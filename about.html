Our story

From coconut husk to a worldwide harvest.

Chanakya Exports turns one of nature's most abundant by-products into premium growing media and industrial coir — and ships it, reliably, to growers and manufacturers around the world.

Who we are

A coir specialist, end to end.

Headquartered in Bengaluru with a dedicated factory in Shengottai, Tamil Nadu — the heart of India's coir belt — Chanakya Exports manufactures and exports a complete range of natural coir products. Coconut husk that would otherwise be discarded becomes coco peat, grow bags, fibre, husk chips and rope.

By controlling the journey from raw husk to finished, palletised container, we keep quality consistent and lead times dependable. We're proud to be recognised among the leading natural coir manufacturers in our market — and to grow alongside the customers we supply.

  • Manufacturer & direct exporter — no middlemen
  • In-house quality control on every batch
  • Flexible to custom specifications and packing
How it's made

From husk to harvest in four steps.

A naturally simple, low-energy process — refined for export-grade consistency.

01 · Sourcing

Mature coconut husks are collected from the groves of South India's coir belt.

02 · Extraction & drying

Fibre and pith are separated, then sun-dried naturally to the right moisture.

03 · Compression & grading

Material is graded and compressed into blocks, briquettes, bags and bales.

04 · Testing & packing

EC, pH and moisture are verified, then loads are palletised and shrink-wrapped.
