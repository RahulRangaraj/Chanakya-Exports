Who we are

Rooted in the coconut groves of South India.

Chanakya Exports is a manufacturer and exporter of natural coir products — by-products of the humble coconut, transformed into world-class growing media and industrial materials.

With a head office in Bengaluru and a dedicated factory in Shengottai, Tamil Nadu — the heart of India's coir belt — we control quality from raw husk to finished, palletised container. Every block, bale and bag is sun-dried, tested for electrical conductivity and pH, and packed to arrive in perfect condition anywhere in the world.

  • Consistent, washed & buffered grades for professional growers
  • Custom dimensions, expansion volumes and packing on request
  • Reliable 40' HC container loads, palletised and stretch-wrapped
